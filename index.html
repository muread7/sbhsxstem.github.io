About

Science Technology Engineering Math

X-STEM is a club in South Brunswick High School. This year there are 2 committees: Makerspace and STEM Awareness. Students part of the Makerspace committee will learn the basics of Image Editing (“Photoshopping”), Computer-Aided Design (CAD), Electronics/Arduino Devices, and Web Design/Programming. The STEM Awareness committtee will research STEM careers, help spread awareness of STEM within the community, and prepare and run activities for middle schoolers.
